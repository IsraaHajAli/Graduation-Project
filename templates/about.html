About Us

We are a team of Computer Engineering students from Birzeit University. This website is our graduation project: Fake News Detection using Machine Learning.

Using NLP and AI, we built a system that analyzes the credibility of news articles. Our goal is to support media literacy and help users navigate the complex world of online information.
