About Us

We are a team of Computer Engineering students from Birzeit University. This website is our graduation project: Fake News Detection using Machine Learning.

Using NLP and AI, we built a system that analyzes the credibility of news articles. Our goal is to support media literacy and help users navigate the complex world of online information.

Why We Chose This Project

In today's digital world, misinformation spreads faster than ever. We believe that technology should empower users, not mislead them. That’s why we developed a tool that automatically detects fake news using deep learning and natural language processing.

Technologies We Used

  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Machine Learning: BiLSTM, DistilBERT
  • Explainable AI (XAI): SHAP
  • Flask, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Browser Extension (Coming Soon)
